Triad — App Summary

Every screen and every button, in plain English.

Pages

  • Splash screen The animated logo that appears when you first open the app, then fades into the home screen after a few seconds.
  • Home The main landing screen — shows a welcome summary, your streak, and quick-access cards for Breathe, Meditate, and Library. First-time visitors see a "Take your First Breath" prompt instead.
  • Techniques A grid of breathing techniques you can browse and tap into for a full description and the option to start a session.
  • Technique detail The detail view for a single breathing technique — description, benefits, and a Start Session button.
  • Meditate A grid of guided meditations you can browse and tap into for a description and the option to start a session.
  • Meditation detail The detail view for a single meditation — description, duration, and a Start Session button.
  • Library A searchable collection of books, people, podcasts, breathing techniques, and meditations organised into tabs.
  • Library detail The detail view for a library entry — a book, person, podcast, or in-depth practice article.
  • Plan (AI) A screen where you answer a few questions about your goals and receive a personalised practice plan from an AI. Requires a Pro account.
  • Profile Your personal dashboard — shows your Triad name, stats snapshot, achievements, Bonsai Garden link, active missions, and account settings.
  • Achievements page A full-screen view of all your achievements, opened from the Profile page.
  • Triad Mastery page A full-screen breakdown of your mastery progress across the three pillars of Triad, opened from the Profile page.
  • Rewards page A full-screen list of rewards you have unlocked, such as the Bonsai Garden, opened from the Rewards button on Profile.
  • Guest Breath Pacer The simplified breathing pacer shown to first-time users — guides you through three breath cycles of Resonant Breathing before completing automatically.
  • Pro Breath Pacer The full breathing pacer for returning users — lets you choose session length, then guides your breathing with a visual pacer and timer.
  • Session screen The active breathing or meditation session view — shows a moving orb, a timer, and pause/resume controls.
  • Session complete screen The screen shown after finishing a breath session — displays your stats and offers buttons to learn more, try a meditation, or go home.
  • Mindfulness of Breath session The dedicated Mindfulness of Breath meditation screen — a physics-based orb you interact with by holding a button, with a countdown timer.
  • Mindfulness of Breath results The results screen shown after a Mindfulness of Breath session — displays breath stats like average breath length and session time.
  • Pre-session intro popup A two-step popup that appears before starting a session — first shows information about the technique, then lets you choose how long to practise.
  • Bonsai Garden A virtual bonsai tree you grow by watering it once a day over 33 days — unlocked after completing your first breath session and first meditation.
  • Bonsai Album A gallery of completed bonsai trees you have grown and saved.
  • Level unlock screen A celebration screen that appears when you reach a new level by completing achievements.
  • Login / Sign up modal The popup for creating an account or logging in, with tabs to switch between the two and a forgotten password option.
  • Settings drawer A slide-in panel with theme toggle (light/dark), sound toggle, and links to privacy information and account deletion.
  • Quit session modal A confirmation popup that appears if you try to leave a session mid-way — gives you the choice to continue or end the session.
  • Quick-start picker A popup showing six curated practices you can jump straight into from anywhere in the app.
  • Pro upgrade modal A popup that appears when you try to use an AI feature without a Pro account, with an option to join the waitlist.
  • API cost confirmation modal A popup that shows the estimated cost of an AI request and asks you to confirm before it proceeds.
  • Edit cycles sheet A bottom sheet with a slider that lets you change the number of breath cycles during an active session.
  • Mission diagnostic A measurement screen used at the start and end of a 21-day mission — either a timed hold test or a short survey.
  • Privacy modal A popup showing privacy and legal information, opened from the Settings drawer.
  • Waitlist modal A popup where you can enter your email to join the Pro waitlist.
  • Achievement popup A small toast notification that appears briefly at the top of the screen when you unlock a new achievement.

Click Events

  • Take your First Breath → Opens the guest breathing pacer with Resonant Breathing.
  • Bottom nav: Home → Goes to the Home screen.
  • Bottom nav: Breathe → Goes to the Techniques screen.
  • Bottom nav: Meditate → Goes to the Meditate screen.
  • Bottom nav: Library → Goes to the Library screen.
  • Bottom nav: Plan → Goes to the Plan screen. Back button returns to Profile.
  • Profile icon button → Goes to Profile if logged in; opens Login / Sign up if not.
  • Gear / Settings button → Opens the Settings drawer.
  • Sticky back bar → Goes back to the previous screen (e.g. from a detail view back to a list).
  • Home: Breathe card → Goes to the Techniques screen.
  • Home: Meditate card → Goes to the Meditate screen.
  • Home: Library card → Goes to the Library screen.
  • Home: Bonsai icon → Opens the Bonsai Garden. Only visible once your garden is unlocked.
  • Home: Log In / Sign Up footer → Opens the Login / Sign up modal (if guest) or goes to Profile (if logged in).
  • Technique card → Opens the detail view for that technique.
  • Technique detail: Start Session → Opens the pre-session intro popup, then launches a breathing session.
  • Meditation card → Opens the detail view for that meditation.
  • Meditation detail: Start Session → For Mindfulness of Breath, opens the dedicated meditation session. For all others, opens the pre-session intro popup.
  • Library tab pills → Switches between Books, People, Podcasts, Breathwork, and Meditations tabs.
  • Library: search box → Filters the current tab's results as you type.
  • Library: theme filter → Filters results by theme.
  • Library: Reset filters → Clears the search box and theme filter.
  • Library item card → Opens the detail view for that book, person, podcast, or practice.
  • Library detail: Back → Returns to the library list.
  • Library detail: Start Session → Launches a session for that practice.
  • Pre-session intro: Next → Moves from the technique information to the session length picker.
  • Pre-session intro: Begin → Closes the popup and starts the session.
  • Pre-session intro: Cancel → Closes the popup and returns to the technique detail.
  • Guest pacer: and Breathe → Starts the 3-2-1 countdown and launches the breathing session.
  • Pro pacer: Pick Breath Work Length → Opens the length selection screen.
  • Pro pacer: Begin Session → Starts the countdown and launches the session.
  • Pro pacer (infinite mode): End Session → Stops the session and shows the completion screen.
  • Session: Pause / Resume → Pauses or resumes the active session.
  • Session: Edit cycles → Opens a slider so you can change how many breath cycles remain.
  • Session: Sound toggle → Turns the breathing audio cues on or off.
  • Session: Close (X) → If the session is running, shows the Quit confirmation. If already complete, closes the session overlay.
  • Edit cycles sheet: Confirm → Applies the new cycle count and closes the sheet.
  • Session complete: Learn about [technique] → Goes to the Library article for the technique you just practised.
  • Session complete: Try a meditation to match → Navigates to Meditate and opens the Mindfulness of Breath session.
  • Session complete: Home → Returns to the Home screen.
  • Mindfulness of Breath: Pick Meditation Length → Opens the duration selection tiles.
  • Mindfulness of Breath: duration tile → Selects that session length.
  • Mindfulness of Breath: Begin → Starts the countdown and opens the active session screen.
  • Mindfulness of Breath: Hold button (press and hold) → Records a breath while held — the orb rises and falls with your hold.
  • Meditation results: Learn about Mindfulness of Breath → Goes to the Library article for Mindfulness of Breath.
  • Meditation results: Home → Returns to the Home screen.
  • Quit modal: Continue → Dismisses the modal and resumes the session.
  • Quit modal: End session → Ends and closes the session.
  • Quick-start picker: practice card → Closes the picker and goes directly to that practice's detail view.
  • Profile: Triad Mastery button → Opens the Triad Mastery sub-page.
  • Profile: View All Achievements → Opens the full Achievements sub-page.
  • Profile: Rewards button → Opens the Rewards page.
  • Profile: Bonsai Garden (in Rewards) → Opens the Bonsai Garden.
  • Profile: Start Mission → Opens the mission diagnostic to record your starting measurements.
  • Profile: Complete Today's Session → Starts the practice session assigned for today's mission drill.
  • Profile: Final Assessment → Opens the mission diagnostic to record your end-of-mission measurements.
  • Profile: Log out → Logs you out and returns the profile to its guest state.
  • Profile: Log In / Sign Up → Opens the Login / Sign up modal.
  • Profile: Claim this Triad name → Saves the suggested name as your permanent Triad name.
  • Profile: Refresh name ↻ → Generates a new Triad name suggestion.
  • Profile: I'll do this later → Dismisses the Triad name onboarding card.
  • Profile: Send Feedback → Submits the text in the feedback box to the team.
  • Profile: Continue Your Daily Practice → Returns to the Home screen.
  • Achievements page: Back → Returns to the main Profile view.
  • Triad Mastery page: Back to Profile → Returns to the main Profile view.
  • Rewards page: Back → Returns to the main Profile view.
  • Plan: Generate (chip selections) → Sends your goal and preferences to the AI and shows your personalised plan. Requires Pro.
  • Plan: Send (follow-up message) → Sends a follow-up question or message to the AI in the plan chat.
  • Plan: Start over → Clears the current plan and returns to the goal selection form.
  • API cost modal: Continue → Confirms the AI request and proceeds.
  • API cost modal: Cancel → Cancels the AI request and restores the previous state.
  • Login / Sign up: Log in → Submits your email and password to log in.
  • Login / Sign up: Create account → Submits your email and password to create a new account.
  • Login / Sign up: switch tab → Switches between the Log in and Sign up forms.
  • Login: Forgot password? → Shows the forgotten password form.
  • Forgot password: Send reset link → Sends a password reset email to the address you entered.
  • Forgot password: Back to login → Returns to the login form.
  • Reset password: Set new password → Saves your new password and switches back to the login form after a moment.
  • Auth modal: Close → Closes the login / sign up popup.
  • Settings: Theme toggle → Switches between light and dark mode.
  • Settings: Sound toggle → Turns session audio cues on or off.
  • Settings: Privacy & Legal → Opens the Privacy modal.
  • Settings: Delete account → Opens the account deletion flow.
  • Pro upgrade modal: Join waitlist → Opens the waitlist signup form.
  • Waitlist: Submit email → Saves your email to the waitlist and confirms you're on the list.
  • Mission diagnostic: Stop timer / Save survey → Records your measurement and closes the diagnostic screen after a moment.
  • Level unlock screen: Start Practising → Dismisses the celebration screen and returns to wherever you were.
  • Bonsai: Plant your seed (Begin Growing) → Names your bonsai and starts growing it. Requires pot and seeds to be unlocked.
  • Bonsai: Water → Waters your bonsai for the day. Can only be done once per day — three waters advance one growth cycle.
  • Bonsai: Save to Album → Saves a picture of your completed tree to your Garden Album and lets you start a new one.
  • Bonsai: Plant New → Discards the current completed tree and starts a new one without saving.
  • Bonsai: Album → Opens the Garden Album to see all trees you have grown.
  • Bonsai Album: Back → Returns to the main Bonsai view.
  • Bonsai: Close → Closes the Bonsai Garden and returns to wherever you opened it from.
  • Bonsai ceremony: Begin Growing → Closes the reward ceremony popup and opens the Bonsai Garden.
  • Bonsai ceremony: Later → Dismisses the reward ceremony popup without going to the garden.
