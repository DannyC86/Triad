Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 May 2026

Triad ("we", "us", "our") is operated by an individual developer based in the United Kingdom. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Triad web application ("the App").

1. Who we are

Data controller: Daniel Clark, United Kingdom. Contact: daniel.clark@live.co.uk

2. What data we collect

Data stored locally on your device only (not sent to us):

  • Your practice sessions, streak, and completion history
  • Your reading list and achievements
  • Your theme preference (light / dark)
  • Your plan and any waitlist sign-up (email address, stored in your browser's localStorage)

Data sent to our servers only when you use AI features:

  • The text of your question when you use "Ask an Expert"
  • The practice context (technique or meditation name) for that question
  • Answers are streamed back and not permanently stored by us

We do not use cookies, analytics trackers, or advertising networks. We do not collect your name, location, or device identifiers.

3. Legal basis for processing (UK GDPR)

When you use the AI Ask feature, we process your question text on the basis of legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) UK GDPR) — specifically, to provide the service you have requested. We have assessed that this does not override your privacy interests given the limited, transient nature of the data.

If you sign up to our waitlist, we process your email address on the basis of consent (Article 6(1)(a) UK GDPR). You may withdraw consent at any time by emailing us.

4. Data retention

  • Local data: stored indefinitely in your browser until you clear your site data or use the reset option.
  • AI query text: processed transiently; not stored on our servers after the response is delivered.
  • Waitlist email: retained until you ask us to remove it.

5. Third-party processors

We use Anthropic, Inc. (USA) to power AI responses. Your query text is sent to Anthropic's API under their published privacy policy. Anthropic is a sub-processor acting on our instructions. International transfers to the USA are made under the UK International Data Transfer Agreement (IDTA) / standard contractual clauses.

We use Railway (USA) to host the application server. Railway processes server logs (IP address, timestamps) for security and operational purposes only.

6. Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the right to: access, rectify, or erase your personal data; restrict or object to processing; and data portability. To exercise any of these rights, email us at daniel.clark@live.co.uk. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the ICO (ico.org.uk).

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Terms of Use

Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before beginning any new breathwork or meditation practice, particularly if you have a cardiovascular, respiratory, or mental health condition.
